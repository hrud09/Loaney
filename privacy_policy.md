# Privacy Policy for Loaney

**Last Updated: March 20, 2026**

Loaney ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application.

---

## 🔒 1. Data Privacy & Local Storage
The most important thing to know about Loaney is that it is a **private, local-first application**.
- **On-Device Storage**: All your financial data, loan records, bank account details, and personal notes are stored **locally** on your mobile device.
- **No Cloud Sync**: We do not maintain any external servers. Your data is never uploaded to the cloud, nor is it accessible to us or any third party.
- **Total Control**: You have complete ownership of your data. You can edit or delete your financial records at any time within the app.

## 📋 2. Information We Access
To provide the app's core features, Loaney requests certain permissions from your device:

### A. Contacts
- **Purpose**: To allow you to quickly select a person's name when creating a new loan entry.
- **Usage**: Contact access is used strictly to pre-fill names and optionally contact numbers. This data remains on your device and is never shared or sold.

### B. Notifications
- **Purpose**: To provide you with timely reminders for upcoming loan deadlines and partial payment schedules.
- **Usage**: You can opt-in or out of these reminders via your device settings.

### C. Storage / Media
- **Purpose**: To allow you to upload and view custom bank cover images or QR codes for your financial accounts.
- **Usage**: The app only accesses specific files you choose to select.

## 🚫 3. Third-Party Services
Loaney does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs that collect your personal information. The app is designed to be a private tool for your personal financial management.

## 🛡️ 4. Data Security
Because Loaney stores data only on your device, the security of your information is tied to the security of your physical device (e.g., your phone's passcode, fingerprint, or face recognition). We recommend using your device's built-in security features to protect your local database.

## 🗑️ 5. Data Retention & Deletion
- **Manual Deletion**: You can delete specific records or all bank accounts within the app.
- **App Uninstall**: Uninstalling the Loaney app or clearing the app’s storage in your device settings will permanently delete all data stored within the app. Since we do not have a server backup, this data cannot be recovered once deleted.

## ⚖️ 6. Children's Privacy
Loaney does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from children. Since all data is held locally on the user's device, we have no way of identifying our users.

## 📝 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time as we add new features. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## ✉️ 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

---
**Note**: This policy is for the Loaney mobile application and does not apply to any third-party websites or services you might use in conjunction with your device.
