# Loaney - Art Asset Generation Prompts

This document contains highly specific prompts for generating the visual assets required to publish the **Loaney** app on the Play Store or App Store. Each prompt is tailored to the app's **Neubrutalist** and **AlimBank** design style.

---

## 🎨 1. App Icon / Logo
**Concept**: A minimalist, bold icon representing a wallet, a coin, or a link in a neubrutalist style.

### Variant A: The Original Neubrutalist Wallet
> **Prompt**: 
> "A high-quality, professional app icon for a financial management app named 'Loaney'. The style is **Neubrutalism** with bold thick black outlines, high contrast, and a flat 2.5D aesthetic. The icon features a stylized wallet merging with a 'plus' (+) and 'minus' (-) symbol to represent lending and borrowing. Color palette: Emerald Green (#00A86B), Cyber Indigo (#6366F1), and Cream (#F5F5F0) on a dark Slate (#101116) background. Minimalist, premium, vector-style, no small text, centered, clean symmetry."

### Variant B: Abstract Geometric Link
> **Prompt**: 
> "A minimalist, geometric app icon for 'Loaney'. Two interlocking squares—one in Emerald Green (#00A86B) and one in Cyber Indigo (#6366F1)—forming a 'link' symbol with thick, chunky black borders. Each square represents a financial node (lender and borrower). **Neubrutalist style**, high contrast, flat 2D design. Bold, premium, iconic, centered on a dark matte background."

### Variant C: Premium 3D Glassmorphism
> **Prompt**: 
> "A premium, semi-transparent **glassmorphic** app icon. A 3D floating digital coin with an embossed 'L' symbol, casting a realistic soft shadow on a dark matte surface. Features frosted glass-blur effects, iridescent glowing edges in Cyber Indigo and Emerald Green, and ultra-fine line-work. Modern fintech aesthetic, high-fidelity 3D rendering, cinematic lighting, sophisticated and luxurious."

### Variant D: Modern Friendly Illustration
> **Prompt**: 
> "A modern, friendly illustration for the 'Loaney' app icon. A stylized, hand-drawn piggy bank safe with a vibrant green 'plus' symbol floating above it. **Neubrutalist influenced** with bold black ink outlines and vibrant flat colors: Emerald Green (#00A86B) and Coral Rose (#FB7185). Playful yet professional, high-quality vector illustration style, perfectly centered on a clean cream background."

### Variant E: Ultra-Minimalist Glyph
> **Prompt**: 
> "An ultra-minimalist, high-contrast glyph logo for 'Loaney'. A single, bold, thick line forming a combined 'upwards' and 'downwards' arrow into a single sharp glyph representing money in-flow and out-flow. Stark contrast: Pure white glyph on a solid Alim Green (#00A86B) background. No outlines, sharp edges, perfect use of negative space. Iconic, timeless, and perfectly balanced."

- **Background Cream**: `#F5F5F0` (AlimCream)
- **Secondary Indigo**: `#6366F1` (CyberIndigo)
- **Warning/Debt Rose**: `#FB7185` (CoralRose)
