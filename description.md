# Loaney - Smart Loan & Bank Manager

**Effortless loan tracking and bank management with a premium, neubrutalist interface.**

## Short Description
Loaney is a modern, visually stunning app designed to help you track personal loans, manage bank contacts, and stay on top of financial deadlines with ease.

## Detailed Description
Loaney is your ultimate financial companion for tracking personal debts and credits. Whether you're lending money to friends or keeping track of your own borrowings, Loaney provides a seamless experience to ensure you never lose track of a single penny. 

Built with the latest Android technologies, Loaney features the unique **AlimBank Design System**—a perfect blend of neubrutalism and glassmorphism that makes financial management feel premium, intuitive, and alive.

### 🚀 Key Features

#### 💳 Advanced Bank & MFS Management
- **Link Everything**: Support for Bank Accounts, Credit/Debit Cards, and Mobile Financial Services (MFS) like bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and Upay.
- **Quick Share**: One-tap copy for account details and mobile numbers.
- **QR Support**: Store and view your MFS QR codes for instant payments.
- **Full Control**: Edit or update your account details anytime. Deletion confirmation ensures your data is always safe.

#### 💸 Seamless Loan Tracking
- **Lend & Borrow**: Document every transaction with names, amounts, and promised return dates.
- **Partial Payments**: Track repayments over time. Loaney automatically calculates the remaining balance and progress.
- **Loan Items**: Add specific items or notes to any loan for better context.

#### 📅 Smart Deadlines & Calendar
- **Upcoming Section**: A dedicated area on the home screen highlights what's due soon.
- **Interactive Calendar**: A 14-day quick-strip and a full featured calendar popup to visualize your financial timeline.
- **Urgency Labels**: "Due Today", "Overdue", and "Due in X Days" keep you informed at a glance.

#### 📊 Financial Insights
- **Balance Snapshot**: View your total lent, total borrowed, and net balance in a beautiful, privacy-protected card.
- **Transaction History**: A comprehensive list of all past and present financial activities.
- **Visual Reports**: Coming soon (Shop/Report section integration).

#### 🎨 Premium User Experience
- **Neubrutalist Aesthetics**: A bold, high-contrast design that stands out.
- **Smooth Interactions**: Bounce effects, haptic feedback, and fluid transitions.
- **Dark/Light Harmony**: Perfectly tuned color palettes for any environment.

## Technical Stack
- **UI**: Jetpack Compose (100%)
- **Architecture**: MVVM with Clean Architecture principles
- **Dependency Injection**: Hilt
- **Local Database**: Room
- **Async Processing**: Kotlin Coroutines & Flow
- **Image Loading**: Coil
